Revaria

Revaria a world filled with Magic, Dragons, the Undead, monsters and everthing in between, it is split into 3 continents, Azpire, Archanous, and Artaria
When you die in Revaria you go to the next world anyone who dies on revaria goes to it it is called the The Azure Plane.
Today Revaria isn't as it used to be with all the moratl races that are fighting one another a new threat looms overhead the races must put aside their differences and work to together to defeat this new threat, and there is only one person who knows what's in store for all of them, Ras'koth.
